Campus Life

There has never been a better time to discover how students who Choose Red benefit from an engaging on-campus experience. Make the most of every moment on campus. Live Red. Here are a few of the best ways to get involved at UCM:

Live On Campus

Living on campus is a great choice and an integral part of the "complete" college experience. With a broad spectrum of opportunities designed to complement your college experience, you will be part of a community where you can grow and experience life independently, yet have support to help you excel on campus. Plus, there is always something happening (visit our Housing facebook, Twitter, and Instagram for all the latest news and events). In support of UCM's Learning to a Greater Degree, UCM requires first-year and sophomore students under the age of 21 to reside on campus as part of a two year residency requirement. Freshmen and sophomores are also required to have specified meal plans.Sign up for on-campus housing today! For more information, contact us at 660-543-4515 or housing@ucmo.edu. View Details

Elliot Union

Welcome to the Elliott Student Union - UCM's campus living room. It's all here for you, whether you are looking to study, play or just catch up with friends. It's your time at UCM and Elliott Student Union is your place. Check out our calendar of events to see what's happening today!

Union Bowling Center

Entertainment

We know how to get the good times rolling at the Union Bowling Center lanes! Our bowling alley is open to the public seven days a week with free parking across the street. Check out our specials to see when glo-bowl and other great deals are available. With bowling and entertaining party packages, you are sure to have a great time at the UCM Union Bowling Center!

Pro Shop

The Bowling Center has a full-service bowling pro shop that offers great options for buying your first bowling ball. Check out our Ball Drilling Services or ask about great bargains on used high-end bowling balls. You can also bring life back to your bowling ball with our Detox Machine.

Reservations

Lanes can be reserved by the hour by calling 660-543-4375 or by filling out this reservation form. Lane reservations must be made 48 hours in advance and are subject to lane availability.

Bowling Leagues

League bowling is an exciting, challenging, and rewarding part of the bowling experience available at the Union Bowling Center. Leagues are enjoyable for beginners, advanced, and even competitive bowlers. Call us to sign up and join in the fun.

Café Rouge

Welcome to Café Rouge, the new coffee house setting in the Union Atrium! Café Rouge serves Jazzman's Coffees, which are Fair Trade Certified from sustainable sources. It features a fireplace with shelves and new furniture, including couches and chairs, bistro-style tables and a laptop bar.

Make a Reservation

With seating for about 30 people, Café Rouge is a venue available for open mic nights, discussion groups and other student events throughout the year. University groups wanting to use the space for public events can reserve it through the office of Meeting and Conference Services.
