Transfer Student

Finish strong. Transfer your credits to the University of Central Missouri. As a transfer student you'll experience a transfer friendly enrollment process, and a hands-on, innovative approach to learning while gaining the skills necessary for success. From future-focused academics to an energetic campus focused on student life, you're sure to find the perfect fit as a UCM transfer student.

Discover multiple ways to learn and grow.

  • UCM offers more than 150 programs to launch your career.
  • Learn from professors that know you by your first name.
  • Join one of more than 200 student organizations.
  • Benefit from free tutoring, writing support and academic enrichment services.

UCM is committed to keeping your college experience affordable. Our commitment provides many opportunities to help you finance your education:

  • Apply for financial aid.
  • Find and apply for a variety of scholarships.
  • Not a Missouri resident? No problem. Enjoy in-state tuition if your home state is one of Missouri's eight bordering states.
  • 15-to-Finish Scholarship available for transfer students who graduate on time
  • Our vibrant campus environment will make you feel right at home.

Graduate School Admissions

Welcome to Graduate School

An applicant for admission to a master's degree program must have a bachelor's degree. An applicant for admission to an Education Specialist's degree program must have a master's degree. Prerequisite degrees must be from institutions accredited by agencies recognized by the University of Central Missouri.

Admission to the Program

Department admission requirements for specific graduate programs are listed in the Graduate Catalog. Some programs require standardized tests, such as the GRE or GMAT, and some require a minimum GPA, letters of recommendation, a statement of purpose, and/or other materials.

Two Steps to Admission

Admission to a graduate program at UCM is a two-part process. First, you must be admitted to Graduate School. If you are admitted to Graduate School, your application is then forwarded to the department containing your graduate degree program. Second, that department then makes a recommendation on admitting you to the degree program. To submit your application, go to our online application form. You will need to pay a nonrefundable application fee and arrange for official transcripts from all colleges or university you have attended to be sent to the Graduate and International Student Services Office.

Please see the University's immunization policy.

Central Missouri Graduate Students (Who Have Not Completed A Graduate Degree) If you are returning to Graduate School at the University of Central Missouri after an absence of a year or more and your degree has not been awarded, please complete a new application. You will not have to pay the application fee for readmission.
